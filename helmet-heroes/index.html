How to Play

  • 🛹 You skate forward by yourself.
  • 👆 Tap (or press Space) when the shrinking ring lines up with the glowing target to land a trick.
  • 🪖 Helmet ON: clean tricks give points and stamina. A missed trick is just a small wobble.
  • 😬 Helmet OFF: a missed trick is a big wipeout — and a police skater starts chasing you!
  • 🔋 Keep the stamina bar out of the red. Combos build a multiplier and a "second wind."
  • ⚡ Helmet flew off! Sometimes your helmet pops off — quickly tap the 🪖 button (or press H) to put it back on. The faster you are, the more energy you get back!
