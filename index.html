📲 Put a game on your iPad

Each game can get its own icon, just like a real app. Do this once for each game:

  1. Open the game in Safari (tap its card above).
  2. Tap the Share button ⬆️ — a square with an arrow pointing up.
  3. Scroll down the list and tap “Add to Home Screen.”
  4. Tap Add. The game’s icon appears on your Home Screen and opens full-screen! 🎉

💡 Use Safari for this (not Chrome). Each game gets its own icon — so add both! 🐾🛹
